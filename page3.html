Fire Parks

Our biggest new idea is creating Fire Parks. Instead of building completely new buildings, Fire Parks can be built as upgraded sections of existing libraries.

Lower Floor

  • Library
  • Computers (if available)
  • Emergency information

Upper Floor

  • Emergency food
  • Water
  • First aid kits
  • Telephone
  • Game boards
  • Resting area

Fire Resistant Design

Uses AAC Panels + FlameOFF Paint


Expected fire resistance: 4–6 hours

Purpose of Fire Parks

Fire Parks create safe areas where people can go during wildfires. They are designed to be nearby, so families do not need to travel far during an emergency.


These buildings would contain emergency supplies such as food, water, first aid kits, and communication tools.

Safety Features

  • Smoke sensors
  • Emergency supplies
  • Fire-resistant construction
  • AAC Panel structure
  • FlameOFF protective coating
  • Spraying pipes if fires last longer
