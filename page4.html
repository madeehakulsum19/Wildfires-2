Why Use SuperClear 45?

The wire mesh inside the glass provides extra strength. It helps hold broken pieces together during damage, improves safety during earthquakes, and provides protection in high-risk environments.


Although this glass costs more than regular windows, the extra protection can help save lives during wildfires.
